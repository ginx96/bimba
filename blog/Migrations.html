7 Reasons Why Migrate from Jira Server to Jira Cloud is Essential for Your Business

Every time I visit my LinkedIn these days, I can’t help but notice the discussions about the "hasty" end of Atlassian's support for Jira Server, which, as expected, will finally arrive after being announced for about 2 years now.

While it's undeniable that this poses a time-sensitive challenge for projects still on the server, I believe it's crucial to consider the key reasons why many of my clients and I have already migrated to the cloud.

Allow me to share these insights with you!

1# Easy access:

Get ready to have a blast with Jira Cloud! Why bother with annoying installations and configurations when you can simply hop onto any web browser and be on your way? No limitations, no restrictions – just pure freedom to work from wherever and whenever you please. So grab your virtual sunscreen and get ready to soak up the rays of productivity!

2# Automatic upgrades: Bid farewell to manual updates!

Jira Cloud automatically updates to the latest version, so you don’t need to worry about maintaining the software or applying updates manually.

3# Scalability: As your business grows, Jira Cloud grows with you.

You can benefit from scalable storage and processing power, accommodating your evolving needs effortlessly.

4# Cost-effective: Say goodbye to hardware and infrastructure costs!

Jira Cloud eliminates the need for hardware and infrastructure costs, as everything is hosted and managed by Atlassian, resulting in cost savings and improved cost predictability.

5# Quick setup: Experience seamless setup in minutes!

Jira Cloud can be set up in minutes, which saves time and resources compared to setting up and maintaining a Jira server on-premises.

6# Security: Rest easy with enterprise-level security features!

Jira Cloud provides enterprise-level security features, including data encryption, user authentication, and secure data storage, ensuring your data remains protected.

7# Integration: Boost productivity and collaboration with seamless integration

Jira Cloud integrates with other Atlassian’s suite of tools like Confluence, Bitbucket, and Trello, as well as third-party applications, which enhances collaboration and productivity.

Bonus# Support

With Atlassian ending support for Server products after Feb 15, 2024, making the transition to Jira Cloud is vital, remember that this end will mean:

  • No technical support
  • No security updates
  • No bug fixes for critical vulnerabilities

Jira Cloud provides dedicated support from Atlassian experts, safeguarding your investments and ensuring a smooth migration.

Summing up! Migrating to Jira Cloud is an imperative step for businesses seeking continuous support, security updates, and bug fixes. Say goodbye to error-prone processes and embrace a future-proof solution. The clock is ticking; it is time to make the move to Jira Cloud today!

So the question is, do you have enough time still to migrate your processes to the Jira Cloud?
